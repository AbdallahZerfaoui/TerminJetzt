Frequently Asked Questions

Is Terminjetzt legal to use?

Yes, Terminjetzt is a perfectly legal tool. We simply monitor publicly available appointment calendars on the immigration office's website. We do not circumvent any security measures or access any private data. We act as an automated notification service, saving you the time and effort of constantly checking for openings yourself. Our service complies with all relevant data privacy regulations.

How can Terminjetzt help me actually get an appointment?

Terminjetzt uses automated monitoring technology to continuously check immigration office websites for appointment availability. When a slot becomes available, our system immediately sends a notification to your email address. This allows you to quickly access the website and book your appointment before someone else does.

Is Terminjetzt free to use?

Yes, Terminjetzt is currently free to use.

How quickly will I receive a notification?

Our system checks for appointment availability continuously. Once an appointment slot is detected, you'll receive an email notification within seconds. The speed of delivery depends on your email provider, but most users receive notifications almost instantly.

What if I don't get a notification? Does that mean Terminjetzt isn't working?

If you haven't received a notification, it means no appointment slots have become available yet. Immigration office appointments can be very competitive, and it might take some time before a slot opens up. Rest assured that our system is continuously monitoring, and you'll be notified as soon as an appointment becomes available.

If you're concerned that the system isn't working properly, you can check your spam folder or contact our support team for assistance.

What happens to my email address? Is my information safe?

We take your privacy seriously. Your email address is only used to send you appointment notifications and essential service updates. We will never sell or share your email address with third parties. You can unsubscribe from our service at any time, and your data will be deleted from our system.

For more information, please refer to our Privacy Policy.
